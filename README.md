# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 15, 2026**

Welcome to **Spinsto**. Your privacy is our absolute priority. This policy explains how our speedometer application handles your information. 

The short version: **Everything stays on your device.**

---

### 1. Local-Only Processing
Spinsto is designed to run entirely locally. We do not maintain servers to collect your data, and there are no background processes that harvest your information. What happens in the app, stays in the app.

### 2. Location Data
To provide accurate speed and distance tracking, the App requires access to your device’s GPS/Location services. 
* **Real-time calculation:** This data is used **only** to calculate your current speed, heading, and distance.
* **No Background Tracking:** We do not track or monitor your location in the background when the app is closed.
* **No External Sharing:** Your coordinates are never uploaded, shared, or sold to third parties.

### 3. Data Storage
We do not use cloud storage or external databases. 
* Any session data or trip statistics are stored strictly in the App's local storage on your physical device. 
* If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently erased from your device.

### 4. Third-Party Access
Spinsto does not contain third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking cookies. Because we do not collect your data, we have no data to sell or trade.

### 5. Permissions
The App may request the following permissions to function:
* **Location:** Required to calculate speed via GPS.
* **Notifications (Optional):** Only used for user-triggered speed alerts.
